Daniel & Hannah Care and Empowerment Foundation (DHCEF) Nurturing Mental Wellness in the Global Christian Community
Daniel & Hannah Care and Empowerment Foundation (DHCEF) is a faith-based non-governmental organization committed to fostering mental well-being within the Christian community worldwide. We provide holistic mental health support, recognizing the interconnectedness of spiritual, emotional, and physical health.

What We Do

Education & Awareness

Our foundation offers workshops and seminars to educate the community on mental health awareness, stigma reduction, and self-care practices.

Community Support Groups

Join our supportive group sessions where you can share experiences and find strength in a community of peers guided by experienced facilitators.

Professional Treatment

We provide professional and confidential counseling services to individuals, couples, and families facing mental health challenges.

Meet the Faculty Members

Committed to fostering mental well-being within the Christian community worldwide through holistic support, therapeutic resources, and spiritual integration.
