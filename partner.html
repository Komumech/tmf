Become a Partner

We invite individuals and organizations to become angel partners with DHCEF. Your partnership, whether financial, spiritual, or through professional counseling support, is crucial to our mission. Your contributions will directly enable us to:

  • Reach and support more individuals in critical need of mental health services.
  • Improve the quality and accessibility of our facilities and programs.
  • Strengthen our team's capacity through ongoing training and development.
