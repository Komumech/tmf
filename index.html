                                    Nurturing Mental Wellness in the Global Christian Community                                
                Daniel & Hannah Care and Empowerment Foundation (DHCEF) is a faith-based                 non-governmental organization committed to fostering mental well-being within                 the Christian community worldwide. We provide holistic mental health support,                 recognizing the interconnectedness of spiritual, emotional, and physical health.            

                   
       
           
           
           
                   
   
   
       
               
           
                           
           
               

                                            Our Vision                                                                 Expanding Reach and Impact                         To meet the growing demand for our services and extend our reach, DHCEF                         Subsidize services for individuals facing financial constraints, ensuring access to                         care regardless of economic status.                         Enhance and expand our facilities, creating more conducive environments for                         healing and recovery.                                        
                                   

           
       
   
   
       
       
           
               

                                            Our Mission                                                                 We aim to educate, empower, and equip individuals within the Christian faith                         to prioritize and manage their mental well-being through biblically-based counseling,                         therapeutic resources, and community support programs. Our goal is to break the stigma                         and integrate mental health fully into spiritual life.
